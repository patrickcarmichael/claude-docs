## Viewing Your Traces


After running your CrewAI application:

1. Log in to your [Maxim Dashboard](https://app.getmaxim.ai/login)
2. Navigate to your repository
3. View detailed agent traces, including:

   * Agent conversations
   * Tool usage patterns
   * Performance metrics
   * Cost analytics
